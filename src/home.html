About the Magazine

This magazine will lead readers to make an informed choice. It can prove to be an eye-opener that can help readers to recognize their potentialities. Readers will be able to gather information about practical learning and its various aspects. This chronicle will be extremely useful for people who are seeking motivation and mentorship in their life.

It includes both contemporary and relevant issues which are explained in simple and lucid language. The magazine is compiled in such a way that it interacts and engages the readers with good animation and content. Positivity and confidence are the essentials of success and this magazine will help in embedding these attributes in our life.

The most pivotal part of a magazine is its content. Speaking of content, Accrescence is designed in a way where information, education, and entertainment go hand in hand. It is an initiative by TDPVista to bring together this family of intellectuals and ignite innovative ideas.
